A Letter to You

From My Heart to Yours

My Love,

As I sit here thinking about this past year, my heart is overwhelmed with gratitude and joy. One year ago, I asked you to be my girlfriend, and you said yes. That moment changed my life forever.

From the first time I saw you at that Yankees game, asking for a portable charger, I knew there was something special about you. Your smile, your laugh, the way your eyes light up when you're excited about something, even when you called me beautiful instead of handsome. I was captivated from the start.

This year has been filled with so many incredible moments. We've traveled together, explored new places, tried new things, and created memories I'll cherish forever. Whether we were hiking in the mountains, going out and dancing at concerts, going to baseball games, or just sitting on the couch watching shows/movies, every moment with you has been a gift.

You've taught me what it means to truly love someone, to support them, to laugh with them, to dream with them. You've shown me patience when I needed it and taught me how to be on time. You've celebrated my successes, and stood by me through challenges. You make me want to be a better person every single day.

I love your adventurous spirit, how you're always up for trying something new. I love your kindness and the way you care for the people in your life. I love your determination and how you chase your dreams. I love your sense of humor and how you can make me laugh even on the hardest days.

Thank you for being my partner in crime, my best friend, my gossip partner, and my love. Thank you for choosing me every day, for believing in us, and for building this beautiful life together.

As we celebrate one year together, I want you to know that this is just the beginning. I can't wait for all the adventures still to come, all the memories we'll create, all the dreams we'll chase together. With you by my side, I know anything is possible.

I love you more than words could ever express.
